The Vision of Future

The vision of a "New Bangladesh" hinges on implementing key reforms across political, administrative, economic, educational, healthcare, and anti-corruption sectors, striving towards a more equitable, responsible, and sustainable nation. First and foremost, political reform must prioritize the establishment of a truly inclusive democracy—it is essential that the interim government ensures fair and transparent elections, enhances the role of opposition parties, and fosters political tolerance.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

I would call upon the international community to engage with ‘new Bangladesh’ anew that aims to realize freedom and democracy, beyond letters, for everyone."

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
